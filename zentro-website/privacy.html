This Privacy Policy explains how Zentro (“the app”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Zentro mobile application.

Information we collect

  • Account information: If you sign in, we may process identifiers needed to authenticate you (for example, email or phone, depending on your sign-in method).
  • Contacts (optional): If you choose to import or invite contacts, we access your contacts only after you grant permission.
  • Audio / microphone (optional): If you use recording features, we access the microphone only while recording and only after you grant permission.
  • Location (optional): If you enable location-based features, we may process your approximate or precise location based on your device settings and permissions.
  • Usage & diagnostics: We may collect app events, crash logs, and performance data to improve stability and user experience.

How we use information

  • Provide core app features (tasks, reminders, syncing, notifications).
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and support.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help operate the app (for example hosting, analytics, authentication, and storage). These providers process information on our behalf and under our instructions.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your choices

  • You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data (see Delete account).

Children

Zentro is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.
