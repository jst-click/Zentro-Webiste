Delete your account

Last updated: 2026-04-16

In-app (recommended)
Open Zentro → Settings → Account → Delete account.
By email
Send a request to Zentro.com@gmail.com from your registered email/phone.

What will be deleted

  • Your account profile (if applicable)
  • Tasks, reminders, and related content stored on our servers (if you use cloud sync)
  • Associated files you uploaded (if any)

What may be retained

We may retain limited data when required by law, for security, or to prevent abuse. If retained, it will be stored securely and only for the required period.

How long it takes

Typical deletion time: up to 7 business days.

Email request template

Copy/paste this into an email:

Subject: Delete my Zentro account

Body:
Hello, please delete my Zentro account and associated data.
My account email/phone: ____________
Device platform: Android / iOS
Thank you.

Replace Zentro.com@gmail.com with your real support email before publishing.
